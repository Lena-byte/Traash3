INTERNATIONAL SCIENTIFIC CONFERENCE

On 25-26 September 2021, the TRAASH research project held, via online platform, the International Meeting for Early Career Scholars:

"In Second Use":
An archaeological and anthropological survey of recycling and reuse in the Greek world.

The meeting was addressed to young researchers (PhD candidates, young PhDs, post-docs) from various fields of the Humanities (archaeologists, historians, folklorists, etc.) who study any aspect of secondary use in the Greek world, in its broadest sense, from prehistoric era to modern times.


THE ORGANIZING AND SCIENTIFIC COMMITTEE
Eurydice Kefalidou (PI), Associate Professor of Classical Archaeology, NKUA
Nikolas Dimakis, Assistant Professor of Classical Archaeology, NKUA
Dr. Merkouris Georgiadis,, Post-Doctoral researcher

SECRETARY
